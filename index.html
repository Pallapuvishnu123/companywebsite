FMCG

Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) represent a category of products that are sold quickly at relatively low prices. These items, including food, toiletries, and cleaning products, are essential in daily life. FMCG is characterized by its high consumer demand, frequent replenishment, and affordable pricing, leading to a swift turnover in the market.

Paliyam Enterprise is a versatile company excelling in the food business, FMCG, arts, school-related activities, recruitment for MNCs, and software development.
