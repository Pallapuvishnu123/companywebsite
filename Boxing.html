About Us:

At KSD School, we take pride in offering both offline and online boxing classes, ensuring accessibility and flexibility for our diverse community. In our dedicated studio, experienced instructors lead invigorating in-person sessions, creating an environment that promotes discipline, strength, and skill development. For those seeking virtual training, our online boxing classes bring the expertise and intensity directly to your home, led by skilled instructors through engaging virtual sessions.Our commitment extends beyond the physical aspects of boxing; we emphasize the values of respect, discipline, and personal growth in each class.
