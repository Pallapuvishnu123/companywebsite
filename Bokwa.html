About Us

Embark on a rhythmic fitness journey with Paliyam Enterprise's offline Bokwa Dance classes at our Madivala studio. Bokwa Dance is not just a workout; it's a celebration of movement and music, and our experienced instructors are ready to guide you through an energizing experience. Join our dynamic and friendly community as we fuse fitness with the joy of dance. Our personalized approach ensures that participants of all levels can confidently step, slide, and shape their way to better health. Experience the invigorating beat, creative choreography, and a supportive atmosphere that makes Paliyam Enterprise's Bokwa Dance classes an exciting addition to your fitness routine. Join us, let loose, and dance your way to a healthier, happier you

Our Programes

Ofline Bokwa Classes

Step into the vibrant world of Bokwa Dance at Paliyam Enterprise's offline classes, hosted at our madiwala studio. Experience the thrill of movement and music as our skilled instructors guide you through dynamic Bokwa routines. Our in-person classes offer a welcoming and supportive atmosphere where participants of all levels can enjoy the fusion of fitness and dance. Join our community at the studio, where you can step, sway, and groove your way to improved cardiovascular health and overall well-being. With personalized instruction and a lively atmosphere, Paliyam Enterprise's offline Bokwa Dance classes provide a joyful and invigorating way to stay active and connected. Let's dance together towards a healthier and more vibrant you!

Online Bokwa Classes
